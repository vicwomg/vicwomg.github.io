About

The Rite Spot is a beloved local spot for dinner, drinks and live music. We are a family owned, woman operated, small business. Thriving in the Mission for almost 70 years, Rite Spot is an ideal stop for an evening of what San Francisco once was and still is, a city of music and art. We offer free live entertainment every night when we are open, paired with comfort food and strong drinks. We appreciate everyone who has continued to support us through the years and these difficult times! We would not be here without the support from our Rite Spot family of musicians/performers, music aficionados, patrons and staff. We look forward to sharing this community in the years to come with both long time loves and new faces.
